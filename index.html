Agustina S. Paglayan


All governments provide some education, but very few provide widespread access to high-quality education.
Why?

As a Ph.D. Candidate in Political Science at Stanford University, this is the central question that motivates my research. To address this question, I draw on insights from political science, development economics, education research, and history.

My work is funded by the Stanford Center for International Development, the Stanford Institute for Innovation in Developing Economies, The Europe Center at Stanford’s Freeman Spogli Institute, and the Stanford Interdisciplinary Graduate Fellowship.
